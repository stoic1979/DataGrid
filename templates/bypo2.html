Analytics Solutions

  • Benchmarking your plant signature data against US plants/units of the same capacity, age, and fuel.
  • Comparing operational damage and start type (hot, warm, cold) against similar units.
  • See where you rank in terms of operations.

Outcomes

  • Determine the relative stresses and damage to key unit components using available signature data (i.e., pressures and temperatures at key points in each unit).
  • Analysis of past reliability and outage data will assist in defining the critical cycling-related components.
  • Procedures for turndown to the lowest loads and how to do that in the least damaging ways.

Benefits

  • Assess performance, establish baselines, and set performance improvement goals
  • Optimize operations and save in operating and maintenance costs.
  • Minimize outages and prioritize systems in your next inspection.
